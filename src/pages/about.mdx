### Purpose

1. We want a tech blog from engineers for engineers. We want to write about things we care about.
2. We want to share practical experience around Cloud, DevOps, Architecture and Software Craftsmanship.
3. We don't want clickbait, hype or marketing fluff.

### Privacy

We respect your privacy. Tech Insights intentionally runs cookieless. We do not set tracking cookies and avoid collecting personal data.
